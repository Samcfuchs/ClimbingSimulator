Infinite Recharge Climbing Simulator

Sam Fuchs (236 Alum)

This tool accurately simulates the geometry and physics of hanging multiple robots in Infinite Recharge! Set a robot's weight to 0 to remove it from the simulation. Try to keep the angle below 8 degrees! Note that the viz constrains the switch to 14.5 degrees of tilt, but still calculates the equilibrium angle if that constraint were not in place.
